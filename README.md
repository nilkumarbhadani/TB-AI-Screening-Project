# 🫁 Breathing Life Into the Forgotten
### AI-Powered TB Screening in Rural India — A Policy Brief

A data storytelling presentation exploring how Artificial Intelligence  
is transforming Tuberculosis detection in rural India.

Created as part of the course **Visualisation and Storytelling with Data**  
at 🎓 **IIT Jodhpur**

---

## 🌍 Why This Topic?

India carries **27% of the global TB burden** — 2.8 million new cases  
every year. Yet most of these people live in villages where the nearest  
clinic is hours away, equipment is broken, and results take days.

This presentation tells that story — through data, through design,  
and through the people behind the numbers.

---

## 📊 What the Data Shows

| Metric | Before AI | After AI | Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Patients Screened | 45,000 | 82,000 | 🟢 +82% |
| 🎯 Detection Accuracy | 78% | 92% | 🟢 +14% |
| ⏱️ Diagnosis Time | 3 Days | 12 Hours | 🟢 −83% |
| ❌ False Negatives | 230 | 85 | 🟢 −63% |

## 💡 Core Message

> *"Prevention through early detection is not charity —  
> it is the most cost-effective public health investment available."*

Every undiagnosed TB carrier infects an average of **10–15 people**  
before treatment begins. The technology to stop this exists.  
The infrastructure exists. The policy decision awaits.
